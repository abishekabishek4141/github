MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni, is one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, Dhoni is admired for his calm demeanor, excellent leadership skills, and lightning-fast wicket-keeping. Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
